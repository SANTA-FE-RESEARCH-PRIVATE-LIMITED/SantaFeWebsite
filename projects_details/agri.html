Optimizing Distribution: Streamlining India's Agri-Food Supply Chain with Smart Routing and Packaging Solution

Task

To develop a robust solution architecture for a capacitated vehicle routing and packaging problem tailored for India's leading Food & Agri Tech Company.

Client

India's Leading Food & Agri Tech Company

Business Challenge

The client is responsible for a massive operation. The challenge lies in optimising routes and packing strategies to ensure timely deliveries while managing vehicle capacities and reducing operational costs.

Disruption Delivered

Our system is specially tailored to handle the high-volume, dynamic distribution needs of the client. This revolutionised the client's supply chain by significantly reducing delivery times, enhancing route efficiency, and providing scalability for future expansion.

Business Highlight

  • Enabled delivering over 2,000 tons of food daily to a network of more than 169,000 retailers.
  • Client reported improvement in retailer's satisfaction, and a substantial reduction in their carbon footprint.

    • Solution Overview

    The implemented solution integrates a state-of-the-art technological framework, which comprises two main components – a vehicle routing system and a bin packing system. The vehicle routing system efficiently manages the fleet, optimally assigns orders to vehicles based on availability and capacity, and dynamically adjusts routes in real-time. The bin packing system ensures that the cargo space is utilized optimally, reducing wastage and enhancing load efficiency.

    Architecture Overview

    The architecture is divided into two interconnected frameworks:

    Order and Vehicle Management



  • Client Portal: Where orders and vehicle updates are initiated.
  • Publisher/API: Facilitates the publishing of order and vehicle events.
  • Order and Vehicle Inventory Services: Process the incoming data.
  • Databases: Separate databases for orders and vehicles store and manage the information

    • Routing and Packing Optimization



    Preprocessing Service: Processes data from databases to prepare it for optimization.

  • Pub/Sub System: A publish-subscribe system for efficient event-driven communication.
  • Cluster Updated Event: Groups orders geographically to optimize routes.
  • Packing and Routing Services: Calculate the most efficient packing combinations and routes.
  • Mapping Service: Visualizes the routes for better understanding and management.
  • Databases: Dedicated databases for routes and mapping ensure data integrity and quick access.
